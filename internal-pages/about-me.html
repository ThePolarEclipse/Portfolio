About me

Introduction

Hi, my name is Richard and I'm a web developer, coder, writer and designer.

History

I've been coding for a decade now, starting from highschool with computer science and learning some PHP to then going to college for games design & development, where I learnt proper coding structure and familiarised myself with C++, C# and JavaScript. After leaving education I took up personal projects that range from game making to creating massive spreadsheets as well as plugins that use Python.

Alongside personal projects, I have been partaking in online courses for web development, mainly the SCS Scheme.

Interests and Hobbies

In my free time I like to be creative. This can range from coding something to designing or even just filling a spreadsheet with information.

I've always enjoyed making things and as I grew up, that manifested into various aspects of my life. For example, I have coded software that can turn a sprite sheet into a gif by reading various files. Another example would be when I created an entire world of stories. I put that all on a spreadsheet but later, as I learnt web development, I transferred that data into a stylised and professional information page.
